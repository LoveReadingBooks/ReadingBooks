Chapter 24: Home Again

第24章：再次回家

Aunt Em had just come out of the house to water the cabbages when she looked up and saw Dorothy running toward her.

艾姆嬸嬸剛從屋裡出來澆捲心菜，這時她抬頭看到桃樂絲向她跑來。

"My darling child!" she cried, folding the little girl in her arms and covering her face with kisses.

“我親愛的孩子！”她喊道，將小女孩摟在懷裡，滿臉親吻。

"Where in the world did you come from?" "From the Land of Oz," said Dorothy gravely.

“你從哪裡來的？”“從奧茲國來的，”桃樂絲嚴肅地說。

"And here is Toto, too. And oh, Aunt Em! I'm so glad to be at home again!"

“托托也在這裡。哦，艾姆嬸嬸！我真高興能再次回家！”
